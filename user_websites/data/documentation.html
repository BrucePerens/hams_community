User Websites Portal Feature Documentation

1. Creating Your Website

To get started, go to your profile and set a unique Website Slug. This will be your personal URL. Then, visit /your-slug/home to create your homepage using Odoo's drag-and-drop editor.

[@ANCHOR: UX_CREATE_SITE] # Tests [@ANCHOR: user_websites:test_group_site_creation] # Tests [@ANCHOR: user_websites:test_group_site_routing]

2. Subscriptions and Updates

Visitors can subscribe to your website to receive weekly email digests of your latest blog posts. You can manage your subscribers from your personal dashboard.

[@ANCHOR: UX_SUBSCRIBE] # Tests [@ANCHOR: user_websites:test_subscribe_to_site] # Tests [@ANCHOR: user_websites:test_subscription_creation] # Tests [@ANCHOR: user_websites:test_unsubscribe_secret]

3. Writing Blog Posts

Sharing your thoughts is easy! Navigate to /your-slug/blog and use the creation tool to write and publish new posts. Your posts are private and can only be edited by you.

[@ANCHOR: UX_CREATE_BLOG_POST] # Tests [@ANCHOR: user_websites:test_tour_create_blog] # Tests [@ANCHOR: user_websites:test_group_blog_post_creation]

4. The Community Directory

The Community Directory is a public list of all users who have chosen to share their websites. You can opt-in or opt-out of this list at any time through your Privacy Dashboard.

[@ANCHOR: UX_COMMUNITY_DIRECTORY] # Tests [@ANCHOR: user_websites:test_tour_community_directory]

5. Exporting Your Data

You have full control over your data. At any time, you can download a complete export of all your pages, blog posts, and reports in a standard format from the Privacy Dashboard.

[@ANCHOR: UX_GDPR_EXPORT] # Tests [@ANCHOR: user_websites:test_gdpr_export_api] # Tests [@ANCHOR: user_websites:test_documentation_route]

6. Deleting Your Content

If you wish to leave the community, you can use the "Delete My Content" feature. This will permanently erase all your pages and posts from our servers, fulfilling your right to be forgotten.

[@ANCHOR: UX_GDPR_ERASURE]

7. Reporting and Moderation

We aim to keep our community safe. If you encounter content that violates our rules, use the "Report Violation" button. Our administrators review all reports and take action when necessary.

[@ANCHOR: UX_REPORT_VIOLATION]

8. Appealing a Decision

If your account has been suspended and you believe it was a mistake, you can submit an appeal through the Appeals Portal. Provide a clear reason for your request, and an administrator will review it.

[@ANCHOR: UX_SUBMIT_APPEAL] # Tests [@ANCHOR: user_websites:test_tour_moderation_appeal]
